## Themes

- **Real products vs memes:** The top 2 by a wide margin ($BNKR at $53M, $CLANKER at $19M) have genuine infrastructure — an AI trading agent and an AI token launchpad. Everything below $5M is either a narrative memecoin, a speculative experiment, or has near-zero active users. The product/meme split is stark.
- **Farcaster as the substrate:** Nearly every token in this list either originated on Farcaster, runs an agent on Farcaster, or relies on Farcaster social data. The health of this ecosystem is entirely dependent on Farcaster's continued growth and adoption.
- **AI narrative aging:** LUM (once $80M) is at $533K. CLAWD is 94.7% below its ATH. The AI agent meme wave of late 2024 / early 2026 has peaked for most tokens. Only BNKR and CLANKER have retained significant value, and both have actual fee-generating products.
- **Notable anomaly — Moltbook absent, Molten present:** The Moltbook (MOLT) token — whose underlying platform was acquired by Meta in March 2026 — is not in the top 10 of the clanker-ecosystem CoinGecko category despite being the most high-profile acquisition story in the ecosystem. Instead, Molten (a rebranded L2 platform) holds the #8 spot with questionable liquidity data. The Moltbook situation represents the most striking recent development in the Bankr/Clanker ecosystem and deserves separate tracking.
